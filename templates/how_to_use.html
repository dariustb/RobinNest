How to Use

A Simple Guide
Step One

Search the Stocks

Go to the "Stock Price" input, enter a price and click the "View Stocks" button. The page will then load all popular stocks worth up to the price you've entered.



Step Two

View the Stocks

Find a stock that you'd like to view and click the "View stock" link under the Dashboard column. This will take you to the stock dashboard.
